Frequently asked questions

How can I integrate this mini drone into my curriculum?

Integrating our mini drone into your curriculum is effortless. We provide comprehensive lesson plans, project ideas, and resources aligned with STEM education standards. Our curriculum covers various topics such as programming, aerodynamics, physics, and robotics, allowing seamless integration into existing lesson plans or extracurricular activities.

Is prior coding experience necessary to use this product?

No prior coding experience is necessary to use our mini drone. We offer user-friendly programming interfaces suitable for beginners, along with step-by-step tutorials and coding challenges to facilitate learning. Advanced users can delve into more complex coding concepts, ensuring that learners of all levels can engage with the product effectively.

Are there safety features built into the drone?

Yes, safety is a top priority in our mini drone design. We have incorporated various safety features to ensure a secure learning environment. These include propeller guards to protect against accidental collisions, altitude hold for stable flight, emergency stop functionalities, and user-friendly controls with multiple flight modes suitable for different skill levels. Additionally, our instructional materials emphasize safe flying practices and guidelines for responsible drone usage.
